First big project I created under Krejta solutions was a project INT. I was tasked with crafting a complete solution for the client that is a major player in the field of construction in the Moravian region. Client requested presentational website that would showcase their range of services and plethora of references. Other then setting the goal I had completely free hands on how I approached to achieve that goal.

I was fully responsible for the complete realization of this project from drafting the wireframe, creating the design from the limited brand sources, the client provided to development of the website.

The final result was heavily modular CMS [Processwire] solution that client was able to manage on their own accords.

Update: Based on this modular custom solution we were able to build multiple microsites [1, 2, 3, 4] for client's subsidiary companies.
