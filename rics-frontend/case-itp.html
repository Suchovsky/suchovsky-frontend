The goal of this project was introduction of new service on the market. I was tasked with creation of wireframe and design of the homepage. Structure of the pages was laid out before hand by the client. The wireframe itself was created in greater detail than was usual with these types of projects which was requested by the client.

Due to high workloads, I was responsible only for the design of the homepage and therefore creation of the basis of the design. After the finalization of homepage, I assigned my colleague to design the remainder of the pages. During this period we had regular meetings with said colleague about the direction of the design.

Update: After the deployment, we A/B tested the landing pages. Based on the results of the tests I suggested design changes to improve user experience.
